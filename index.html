How to Use Myth Maker

Welcome to Myth Maker! Here's how to get started:

  1. Navigate to Home: Click on "Home" to see featured stories. You can select any story to start reading.
  2. Explore the Library: Click on "Library" to view all the stories you have in your collection.
  3. Read a Story: Once you select a story, read the dialogue and make choices by clicking on the available options. Your choices will influence the story's outcome.
  4. Track Your Progress: The progress bar at the bottom of the story section shows how far you've progressed in the story.
  5. Enjoy the Adventure: Immerse yourself in the interactive stories and enjoy the adventures!

If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to us. Happy reading!
